How to Delete Your Earnzo Account

Warning: Account deletion is permanent. All your data, including profile information and earnings history, will be permanently removed.

Steps to Delete Your Account:

1. Open the Earnzo App

Launch the Earnzo app on your device and make sure you're logged in.

2. Go to Profile

Navigate to your profile section by tapping on the profile icon.

3. Find Delete Account Option

Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" button at the bottom of your profile page.

4. Confirm Deletion

Tap on "Delete Account" and confirm your choice when prompted. You may need to enter your password for security.

Note: If you're having trouble deleting your account through the app, please contact our support team at support@earnzo.com
